Bio

I am a passionate and dedicated professional in the field of computer science, with a strong academic background and extensive industry experience. My journey in the world of technology began at Babes-Bolyai University in Cluj-Napoca, where I pursued my undergraduate degree in Computer Science.

Driven by my curiosity and a thirst for knowledge, I continued my educational journey by enrolling in a master's program in Software Engineering. This provided me with a deeper understanding of the intricacies of software development and engineering principles.

In parallel to my academic pursuits, I embarked on a professional career with a renowned multinational company based in Switzerland. Working within this dynamic and innovative environment has not only honed my technical skills but also exposed me to the global landscape of technology and business.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me, and I look forward to connecting with you on our shared journey of exploration and discovery in the world of technology.
